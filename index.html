About SWAGATH TECH SYSTEM

Leading plumbing and electrical contracting company in Karnataka since 2015 - Official Partner of ANUV Engineers

Dharnappa Purusha

Founder & CEO

Dharnappa Purusha is a diploma graduate from KP Mangalore with over 10 years of experience in the industry. He worked at Infosys Mangalore for 5 years and gained valuable experience working with various companies before starting his own business. In 2015, he founded SWAGATH TECH SYSTEM and established a successful partnership with ANUV Engineers, specializing in plumbing and electrical contracting projects.

As the official plumbing and electrical contractor for ANUV Engineers, we handle all their construction projects' plumbing and electrical requirements. This partnership allows us to showcase the same high-quality projects while maintaining our specialized focus on plumbing and electrical systems.

His vision is to provide high-quality, reliable plumbing and electrical services while maintaining the highest standards of safety and customer satisfaction. Under his leadership, SWAGATH TECH SYSTEM has become a trusted name in Karnataka for all types of plumbing and electrical contracting needs.
